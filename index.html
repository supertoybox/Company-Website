Super Company is small, dedicated team that helps startups plan, design and build products. We believe that design isn't just how a product looks but, also how it works and how it feels. We focus very much in execution and understand that it can be the little details that make a mundane experience into magical one.

We have the experience and knowledge as startup founders ourselves in what it takes and what is needed to bring an idea to life. We are unlike traditional agencies and are very hands on thoughout the entire process. We very much like to work closely with our clients and not for them.

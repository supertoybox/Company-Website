Super Company is a small, dedicated team helping startups turn an idea on a moleskin page into an app in the app store. We help concept, plan, design, and build your product with you, to get you and your team to the next stage as quickly as possible. We specialize in building rapid prototypes and MVP's, but we can also build your fully realized app, and help you take it to market.

We are not a traditional agency studio, we are hands on collaborators, from ideation to execution, with experts to help with every stage of the development process. We specialize in the rapid development of initial prototypes to take your ideas from paper to the real world with solid builds that can evolve and scale as you grow. We'll get you ready to pitch investors, raise money on Kickstarter, or publish into the app stores.
